Our Twofold Principles

1. Protections

This agreement is built to protect our shared vision. It safeguards our intellectual property, defines our mutual commitment to high-impact work, and ensures we maintain the final authority on the company's direction.

2. Compensations

We believe in transparent and fair compensation. This agreement provides a clear path to earning meaningful equity and ensures that every hour of contribution is valued and accounted for through a deferred wage system.

Our Protections Framework

This section outlines the core protections that safeguard our company's assets, culture, and competitive edge. Click each title to learn more.

"IP" is defined as a specific set of proprietary assets critical to the business. This includes proprietary user data, customer lists, uniquely created content, and trade secrets. This expressly excludes a contributor's general skills, experience, and knowledge, as well as general software code or industry practices not proprietary to our core IP.

Both founders and contributors agree to a mutual commitment to consistent, high-impact work. Contributions will be evaluated quarterly. If commitments are not met, we reserve the right to re-evaluate roles and terms.

This engagement is at-will, meaning either party may terminate it at any time, with or without cause. Termination does not affect any vested equity or earned deferred wages.

To protect our team and customer relationships, contributors agree not to solicit employees or customers for a period of twelve (12) months after their departure for competitive purposes.

While general coding skills are yours, code, algorithms, or methodologies developed specifically for the company cannot be used on a directly competing product or service within the same industry for twelve (12) months after departure.

This agreement serves as a living document of our shared commitment.
